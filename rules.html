OUR RULES

1. Eligibility & Registration - Open to singers aged [13+]. All contestants must register before the deadline and agree to these rules.


2. Performance Guidelines - Songs must be performed live (no lip-syncing), family-friendly, and within [2-4 minutes]. Backing tracks or live instruments are allowed.


3. Judging & Voting - Performances will be judged on vocal ability, stage presence, and originality. Audience votes may count, but judges' decisions are final.


4. Conduct - Respect towards judges, staff, and fellow contestants is required. Unprofessional or disruptive behavior may lead to disqualification.
